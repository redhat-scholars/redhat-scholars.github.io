link:https://www.redhat.com/en/technologies/cloud-computing/openshift/serverless[OpenShift Serverless] means focusing on the business logic regarderless of servers!

link:https://openshift-knative.github.io/docs/docs/latest/index.html[OpenShift Serverless Logic] is in a Developer Preview phase. One of the first capabilities that will be released is the *workflow engine* which helps developers to tackle complex microservices orchestration problems.


What you will learn:

* Serverless Workflow
* CloudEvents
* Develop workflows leveraging Quarkus
