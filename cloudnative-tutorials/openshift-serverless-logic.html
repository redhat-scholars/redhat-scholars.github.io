Duration

Audience

Level

OpenShift Serverless means focusing on the business logic regarderless of servers!

OpenShift Serverless Logic is in a Developer Preview phase. One of the first capabilities that will be released is the workflow engine which helps developers to tackle complex microservices orchestration problems.

What you will learn:

  • MicroServices Orchestration

  • Serverless Workflow

  • CloudEvents

  • Develop workflows leveraging Quarkus
